Accomodation

Please see below for details on Camping, Hotels and Other Accommodation in Devon.

Camping

We would love for you to camp with us after the reception at John's parents'
meadow in North Tawton.

The Church is a 20 minute walk from the meadow, and you are welcome to park
your car there. We will be providing a bus back in the evening.

Please let us know in your RSVP if you would be interested in camping, and we will send out extra details

Hotels

There are plenty of hotels in Exeter, the closest to Double Locks are:

  • Premier Inn (Countess Wear)- 30 minute walk/10 minute drive
  • The Devon Hotel- 33 minute walk/10 minute drive

Some hotels in Central Exeter are as follows:

  • Premier Inn (City Centre)
  • Holiday Inn Express (City Centre)
  • Hotel du Vin
  • Jurys Inn

These are all around a 15 minute drive from Double Locks and 45-60 minute walk.

Other Accomodation

If you would like to stay in North Tawton, but don't wish to camp, then we would recommend looking on Airbnb

There are also plenty of towns and villages between Exeter and North Tawton with holiday cottages available.
